Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

PolishPay ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

Account data

When you sign up, we collect your phone number. We do not collect your legal name, email address, or payment information during sign-up.

Commission data

We store the service entries you log: customer names, services performed, prices, dates, and notes. This data belongs to you.

Salon data

If you create a salon (owner account), we store your salon name and a generated invite code. If you join a salon (employee account), we store the association between your account and the salon.

Usage data

We do not currently collect analytics or usage tracking beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the app — authenticate your account, store your entries, and calculate commission summaries
  • To process payments — if you upgrade to an Owner account, we use Stripe for billing (see below)
  • To communicate with you — if you contact us, we respond via email

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase, hosted on AWS in the United States. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

4. Payment Processing

Owner subscriptions are processed by Stripe. When you subscribe, you are subject to Stripe's privacy policy. We do not store your full credit card number — Stripe handles all payment data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider
  • Stripe — payment processing for owner subscriptions
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or court order

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing hello@polishpay.app. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

You can export your commission data at any time using the CSV export feature in the app.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed.

8. Children's Privacy

PolishPay is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. If we make material changes, we will notify you via the app. Continued use of PolishPay after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
