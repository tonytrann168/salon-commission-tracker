How it works

Simple for techs. Powerful for owners.

Every person in your salon uses it differently — and that's by design.

📱

Techs log their own clients

After each service, the tech opens PolishPay and logs the customer, service, and price. Takes 15 seconds. No paper, no chasing people down at end of day.

📊

Owners see the full picture

Your dashboard shows every tech's earnings for the week or pay period — broken down by service type. You know who's busy, who isn't, and exactly what you owe.

💸

Export and pay

One tap exports a CSV with every entry — customer, services, totals by tech. Bring it to your accountant or use it to cut checks directly.

Everything you need

Built around how nail salons actually run.

Not a generic "salon suite" with booking, retail, loyalty, and 40 other things you'll never use.

Payroll
Commission breakdowns by pay period

Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — set it once and PolishPay calculates each tech's total automatically, including take-home based on your commission rate.

Team
Owner + employee accounts

Invite techs with a 6-character code. Each tech sees only their own calendar. The owner sees everyone. No shared passwords, no confusion.

Appointments
Book clients, assign to techs

Owner books an appointment and assigns it to a tech. The tech sees it on their calendar. When they complete the service, it logs automatically.

Export
CSV export for payroll

Export all entries as a spreadsheet — organized by tech, date, and service. Ready to hand to your accountant or import into QuickBooks.
